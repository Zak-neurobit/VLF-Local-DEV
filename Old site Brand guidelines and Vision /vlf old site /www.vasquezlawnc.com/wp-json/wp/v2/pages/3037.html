Fall 2024 Winner Jennifer Guzman Millan<\/h2>\n

Jennifer is a hardworking graduate student at the University of Michigan-Flint, where she is pursuing her doctorate in Occupational Therapy. We encourage you to read her beautiful poem, which illustrates her life as an ambitious immigrant student in search of her next opportunity.<\/p>\n

Read Jennifer’s Poem<\/h3>\n

A Door of Opportunity<\/h4>\n

As an \u201cillegal alien\u201d, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As the eldest child of her parents, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As the oldest and only undocumented sibling, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As an older sister who encourages her little sisters to surpass her, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As one that has lived her whole life with many \u201cfirsts\u201d, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As the pillar of the family due to her language and educational skills, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As one who was depended on and couldn\u2019t ask for help growing up, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As one who was rejected from many things due to her status, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As one who always found something she was very qualified for but read \u201cMust be US citizen\u201d, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As one who was told not to pursue higher education by educational professionals because of her status, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As one whose undocumented parents always believed in her and are willing to work extra hard for their daughter to become a health professional one day, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As one who carries the burden of always trying to prove that I belong here to others; and that I am a human with hopes, dreams, and aspirations, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As a high-achieving student who completed all her education in the US, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As someone who had many doors closed to her face and others not opened at all, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As a proud Mexican-born student who has been putting triple the effort of others to hopefully one day be able to complete the last stage in her education, I am looking for a door<\/p>\n

As someone who is very well deserving of a door, I will continue looking for a door<\/p>\n
