Judith Benzaquen Parkes<\/span> was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico, and is fluent in both Spanish and English. From an early age, Judith showed a strong interest in the legal field, which led her to excel academically and build a solid career in law.<\/span>\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n

Judith received her Juris Doctorate from the University of Puerto Rico School of Law, graduating with honors (Cum Laude). She is currently a Board-Certified Attorney of the Supreme Court for the states of California and Puerto Rico.<\/span>\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n

She began her legal career at the Puerto Rico Department of Justice, where she served as a Legal Advisor to the Secretary of Justice on legislative matters. Judith also gained international experience as a member of the U.S. Envoy to Mexico team at the U.S. Embassy in Mexico City.<\/span>\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n

Judith developed a passion for Immigration Law when she participated in the Immigration Clinic at her law school, gaining a deeper understanding of the humanitarian aspects of immigration. She later opened her private practice in San Diego, California, focusing on family petitions, citizenship, adjustment of status, and removal defense.<\/span>\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n

Two years ago, Judith relocated to Orlando, Florida, and began working at Vasquez Law Firm. She provides high-quality legal representation to individuals in Removal Proceedings before the Immigration Court and United States Citizenship and Immigration Services. Judith strives to offer honest and professional representation, with the sensitivity and compassion needed to guide clients through life-changing immigration processes.<\/span>\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n

In her free time, she enjoys hiking, exploring the city with her four-legged companion Dexter, and spending time with her family.<\/span>\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n
